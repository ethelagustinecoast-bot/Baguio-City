Discover Baguio

Welcome to Baguio City — a city where history, culture, and stories come alive! Known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines, Baguio charms visitors with its cool mountain breeze, breathtaking views, and rich traditions that make every visit unforgettable.

Baguio was developed as a hill station during the American era, with landmarks like The Mansion and Burnham Park still standing today. Its culture comes alive through locals who keep traditional crafts like weaving, woodcarving, and painting—blending them with modern creativity.

Baguio is where history and modern life come together! Discover the stories hidden in every corner while soaking up the city's rich culture and colorful energy.
